Our
Story

Three decades of uncompromising craftsmanship, timeless elegance, and quiet confidence.

1994

The Beginning

"I didn't want to create fashion. I wanted to create something you'd pass down to your daughter."

Maison Élite was founded in Paris in 1994 by Isabelle Maison, a visionary designer who believed that true luxury should outlast every trend. In her small atelier near the Marais, she created the first collection of twelve pieces — each one a meditation on simplicity, quality, and the power of restraint.

Thirty years later, that philosophy remains unchanged. Every collection is a dialogue between heritage and modernity, between the handcrafted and the architectural, between the intimate and the grand.

Paris · London · Milan · New York

A Message from Our Founder

"I started Maison Élite because I believed the world needed less clothing, but better clothing. Clothing that honours the person who wears it."

When I opened the doors of our first atelier in Paris, I had thirty metres of the most beautiful silk I had ever touched, twelve designs, and an unshakeable belief that fashion could be both timeless and meaningful.

Three decades later, I am more convinced of that than ever. Each collection is my love letter to the art of dressing well — with integrity, with intention, and with joy.

— Isabelle

Frequently Asked

Common
Questions

How do I know my size? +
Our size guide is available on every product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our size chart. If between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a relaxed, Parisian fit. Our team is also available to provide personal styling advice.
What is your return policy? +
We offer complimentary returns within 30 days of purchase for all full-price items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached. Sale items are final sale. Initiate your return through your account or contact us directly.
Do you offer personal styling? +
Yes. We offer complimentary personal styling consultations in our Paris boutique and virtually. Our stylists will curate a selection based on your wardrobe, lifestyle, and preferences. Book your appointment through our contact form.
How do I care for my pieces? +
Care instructions are included with every garment and listed on each product page. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for silk and delicate pieces, gentle machine wash for linens, and professional pressing to maintain shape. Never tumble dry luxury fabrics.
Do you ship internationally? +
We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International orders typically arrive within 5-10 business days. All international orders include full tracking. Customs and import duties may apply depending on your country — these are the responsibility of the customer.
